Habit Garden Site Plan

Yin He

WDD 131-03

Overview

Purpose

To help users track their progress in developing good habits, and to encourage consistency through a gamified experience where habit growth is visualized through increasing levels.

Audience

Individuals seeking to establish positive habits and visually track their progress across multiple distinct routines.

Dynamic elements

JavaScript will dynamically track habit progress and update the display with different emojis to represent the growth stages.

Content

Home page

This page presents a gallery of interactive habit cards. The text content will include a brief welcome message, a description of the current user's habit garden, and labels for each habit card, such as habit name, goal description, and growth stage.

Introduction

This page provides a detailed reference for the Habit Garden system. The text will explain the 21-day theory for establishing new habits, describe how different emojis represent various stages of habit development (from seed to fruit), and offer encouraging text to motivate users on their journey.
